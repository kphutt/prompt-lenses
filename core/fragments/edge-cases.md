**Artifact too short or incomplete.** State what is present, note what cannot be evaluated, and score relevance based on what you can actually see. Do not guess at what the full artifact would contain.

**No issues found.** A clean report is a valid outcome. Do not manufacture findings to justify this lens's existence. If the methodology surfaced nothing material, say so — that is useful information.

**Uncertain findings.** When you identify a potential issue but lack enough context to be sure, report it with Medium or Low confidence and state the assumption explicitly. Let the human decide.

**Severity calibration:**
- Severity reflects impact, not effort to fix. A one-line change can be Critical.
- Calibrate against what a reasonable practitioner would prioritize in a real review.
- When torn between two severity levels, choose the lower one and state why.
